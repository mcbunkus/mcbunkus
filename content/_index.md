# Hi, I'm Austen 👨🏽‍🚀

I'm a recent graduate from Auburn University where I studied Aerospace engineering. During my undergrad, I was a member of Auburn's rocketry team where I competed in NASA's USLI competition and in Spaceport America Cup. After I graduated I worked for a startup called Remora Robotics developing an autonomous boat that cleans trash from waterways. Here I worked on prototyping circuit boards, developing simulation environments with Blender, and did most of the software development. I programmed everything from the microcontrollers on the drone, the ROS nodes running on the drone, to the web applications we used. I currently work at Torch Technologies as a software engineer.

This site is my resume/portfolio/blog, and I write about projects I'm working on or things I'm learning about. I think that writing about problems and working through solutions is a great way to solidify topics and understanding.
