# Hi, I'm Austen 👨🏽‍🚀

I'm a recent Aerospace grad from Auburn University. While in school, I was a
member of Auburn's rocketry team where I competed in NASA's USLI competition and
in Spaceport America Cup. After I graduated I worked at a startup called Remora
Robotics, developing an autonomous boat that cleans trash from waterways. Here I
worked on prototyping circuit boards, modeled simulation environments, and did
most of the software development. I programmed everything from the
microcontrollers which talked to the sensors, developed the ROS nodes running
the drone, up to the web applications we used. 

I currently work at Torch Technologies as a software engineer, where I work in
HWIL missile simulations. This site is my resume/portfolio/blog, and I write
about projects I'm working on or things I'm learning about.
